Everything You Need for Global Awareness

Comprehensive intelligence gathering and analysis tools in one unified platform

🌍

Dual Map Engine

Switch between photorealistic 3D globe and WebGL flat map. 45+ toggleable data layers including conflicts, military bases, infrastructure, and real-time events.

🤖

AI-Powered Analysis

Local LLM support with Ollama/LM Studio. AI-synthesized briefs, threat classification, focal point detection, and country instability scoring.

📰

170+ News Feeds

Curated RSS feeds across geopolitics, defense, energy, tech, and finance. Smart categorization with real-time updates and breaking news alerts.

📡

Live Video Streams

9+ default channels including Bloomberg, Sky News, Al Jazeera. 70+ additional sources available. HLS native streaming with fullscreen mode.

🛰️

Satellite & Infrastructure

Track military flights, naval vessels, undersea cables, pipelines, datacenters, and critical infrastructure. Real-time AIS and ADS-B data.

📊

Market Intelligence

7-signal macro radar, crypto prices, BTC ETF flows, stablecoin health, Fear & Greed Index. Gulf economies panel with live market data.

🌐

21 Languages

Fully localized interface with native-language RSS feeds. RTL support for Arabic and Hebrew. AI translation for cross-language intelligence.

⚡

Real-Time Monitoring

Sub-60-second refresh on critical feeds. Custom keyword monitors, regional alerts, and multi-signal anomaly detection with baseline comparison.

💻

Native Desktop App

Tauri-based desktop application for macOS, Windows, and Linux. Local API sidecar, OS keychain integration, and offline map support.
